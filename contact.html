Frequently Asked Questions

How can I report a bug in your games?

Use the contact form above and select "Bug Report" as the subject. Please include details about the game, platform, and steps to reproduce the issue.

Do you offer internships?

Yes! We regularly offer internship programs. Check our careers page or send us a message through the contact form.

Can I submit a game idea to you?

We appreciate creative ideas, but we currently don't accept unsolicited submissions. However, we're always open to discussing partnerships with established studios.

Where can I find press kits for your games?

Press kits are available upon request. Select "Press & Media" in the contact form and we'll send you the relevant materials.
